About MedStocksy - Smart Pharmacy Inventory Management & CRM Platform

MedStocksy is a cloud-based pharmacy inventory management system and customer relationship management (CRM) platform designed specifically for pharmacies, medical stores, and healthcare retailers across India. Since our launch, we've been committed to transforming how pharmacies manage their operations—from stock tracking to customer relationships—with an intelligent, user-friendly platform built by pharmacy professionals, for pharmacy professionals.

We understand the unique challenges that pharmacy owners and managers face daily: managing thousands of medicine SKUs, preventing stock-outs and wastage, tracking expiry dates across multiple batches, maintaining patient records, and building long-term customer loyalty. MedStocksy is the pharmacy management software that solves all of these problems in one integrated platform.

Smart Pharmacy Inventory Tracking

Our core strength is real-time pharmacy stock management. MedStocksy helps you:

  • Receive automatic low-stock alerts before you run out of critical medications
