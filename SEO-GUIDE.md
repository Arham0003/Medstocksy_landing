# SEO Optimization Guide for MedStocksy

## Files Created:
1. `sitemap.xml` - XML sitemap for Google and Bing
2. `robots.txt` - Search engine crawling instructions
3. Enhanced meta tags and structured data in all HTML pages

## How to Submit Your Sitemap to Search Engines:

### 1. Google Search Console
1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console/)
2. Add your property: `https://medstocksy.in`
3. Verify ownership (usually automatic for Vercel domains)
4. Navigate to "Sitemaps" in the left menu
5. Add your sitemap URL: `https://medstocksy.in/sitemap.xml`
6. Click "Submit"

### 2. Bing Webmaster Tools
1. Go to [Bing Webmaster Tools](https://www.bing.com/webmasters/)
2. Add your site: `https://medstocksy.in`
3. Verify ownership
4. Navigate to "Configure My Site" → "Sitemaps"
5. Submit your sitemap: `https://medstocksy.in/sitemap.xml`
